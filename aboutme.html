Craiova

Craiova is Romania's 6th largest city and capital of Dolj County. Craiova it is situated near the east bank of the river Jiu in central Oltenia and located at approximately equal distances from the Southern Carpathians (north) and the River Danube (south). Craiova is the chief commercial city west of Bucharest and the most important city of Oltenia.
Here below I will name you some of the places you can visit in Craiova:

  • Botanical Garden
  • Nicolae Romanescu Park
  • Museum of Oltenia
  • Craiova Art Museum
  • The Church of Cosuna Monastery
  • St. Demetrius Cathedral etc.


Bibescu Park is located on the place where, in the middle of the nineteenth century, there was the estate and garden of the Bibescu family. The garden, arranged by a German gardener,
at the initiative of the great chancellor Ioan Iancu Bibescu, was arranged with pavilions, benches, greenhouses.
After 1848, being in an advanced state of degradation, the garden was bought by the Magistrate of the city. The purpose of the purchase being to turn it into a real public garden for the
recreation of the inhabitants of the city.

In 1898 Nicolae Romanescu, mayor at that time where a modernization project of the city is voted, one of the objectives of the program being the establishment of parks and gardens.
The project was designed by French architect Edouard Rendont. Edouard Redont took the project of the future Romanescu Park to the International Exhibition in Paris (1900), and the project was
awarded the Gold Medal.

The construction of the park began in 1901 and was completed in 1903. The park was made in romantic style, a synthesis of architecture and landscape painting. Romanescu Park was inaugurated in september 29, 1903 in the presence of King Carol.

At today Romanecu Park it is the most beautiful and large park in Craiova, being one of the most beautiful in the country. Romanescu Park it is spread over an area of 96 hectares and is considered to be the largest natural park in Eastern Europe. There are a lot of extremely attractive things at this park, such as the fact that it is equipped with a zoo, a hippodrome and even an amphitheater for shows. Those who visit it can discover other interesting objectives, such as the Suspension Bridge or the Enchanted Castle.
